Morocco

Located in the North West of Africa, Morocco was the country I visited on my first girls holiday. Upon arrival we were treated like royalty which had lasted the whole time we were there. During our time there we took advantage of the many great acitivites, cuisines and local traditions. a few of the activities included: jet skiing, quad biking, camel riding and much more. In the evenings the city of Marakech would light up with markets, dancers and entertainment all around.
